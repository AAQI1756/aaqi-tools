Disclaimer: This calculator is a tool to assist in estimating your Zakat obligation. It is based on a standard calculation of 2.5% of net assets held for one year. For a precise and accurate calculation, it is highly recommended to consult a qualified religious scholar or an Islamic finance expert.

Note: Distribution of Zakat is designated for eight categories, as mentioned in the Quran (Surah At-Tawbah, 9:60):

  • The poor (Al-Fuqara)
  • The needy (Al-Masakin)
  • Zakat administrators (Al-Amileen)
  • Those whose hearts are to be reconciled (Al-Mu'allafatu Qulubuhum)
  • For freeing slaves (Ar-Riqab)
  • Those in debt (Al-Gharimin)
  • In the cause of Allah (Fi Sabilillah)
  • The wayfarer (Ibn As-Sabil)
